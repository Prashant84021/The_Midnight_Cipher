Chapter 3: A Killer’s Game
Serena confronted Halstrom, now in his eighties, but his dismissive attitude enraged her. “That case was buried years ago,” he said. “It has no relevance now.”

The killer struck again, this time leaving a video message encoded with coordinates. Nate decoded them, leading Serena to the university. She was playing the killer’s game now, racing to prevent another murder.
