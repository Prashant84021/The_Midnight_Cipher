In the lecture hall, Serena found Rebecca Grant. Rebecca confessed with tears in her eyes. Her father had spent his life seeking justice for Clara, but no one had listened.

“I had to make them see,” Rebecca said, her voice shaking. “Halstrom killed Clara, and they let him walk free.”

Rebecca’s actions were monstrous, but Serena couldn’t deny the truth behind her words. Arresting Rebecca felt hollow, knowing the system had failed Clara—and Rebecca too.
